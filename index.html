Search tips

Not finding what you need in CONSORT? Try OhioLINK or WorldCat.
Or, order a book from our Interlibrary Loan service.

CONSORT Search Tips:

Start with a keyword search because a keyword search will look for your terms in titles, authors, subjects, and tables of contents.

Use the menu to limit to title, author, or subject when you want CONSORT to check only those fields for your terms.

After searching, you can limit your results to items held here at Ohio Wesleyan if you wish by clicking on the All Libraries menu and selecting Ohio Wesleyan.
