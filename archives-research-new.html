Research Request Form

Your Information:

There is a flat $25 fee for genealogical research. No fee will be charged for East and West Ohio Conference historians, ministers, or agencies requesting non-genealogical research assistance. Payment is due once you have received completed research. Genealogical research is done as time permits, in the order that requests are received. In general, researchers can expect to wait two to three months for completed research.


I agree to pay the $25 fee upon receipt of completed research and understand that there is a possible wait of two to three months.
