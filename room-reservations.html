Room Reservations

Beeghly Library is used by groups across campus for classes, meetings, lectures, and other events. Rooms are available on a first come/first serve basis, and events must be affiliated with the university.

Rooms can be reserved through the campus reservation system at http://schedule.owu.edu/

Available rooms include:

ICEcube

The ICEcube is ideal for small group active learning, collaboration, or presentations. The room is equipped with a large flat-panel display, conference table, built-in power, and a large whiteboard. For more information, see the guidelines for use.

ICEcube

The Bayley Room

The Bayley Room seats 100 people. It is available for campus-related events, presentations, or large meetings.

The Bayley Room

Great Teachers Room

The Great Teachers Room seats approximately 60 people. Special permission from the library director is required to reserve this space.

Great Teachers Room

Group Study Room 2

Group Study Room 2 seats up to 10 people. The room is great for small group study sessions and collaborative work.

Group Study Room 2

Group Study Room 3

Group Study Room 3 seats up to 10 people. The room is great for small group study sessions and collaborative work.

Group Study Room 3

Media Center Rooms

The Media Center offers classrooms for instruction sessions as well as video editing suites equipped with iMacs and screening equipment. Rooms in the Media Center may be reserved via the Media Center Reservation Request form.

Media Center Rooms

For questions, or more information, contact Bonnie Mahle at bhmahle@owu.edu or 740-368-3247.
