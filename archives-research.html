Archives of Ohio United Methodism

Research Request Form

Your Information:

There is a flat $25 fee for genealogical research. Payment is due once you have received completed research. Genealogical research is done as time permits, in the order that requests are received. In general, researchers can expect to wait two to three months for completed research.

No fee will be charged for East and West Ohio Conference historians, ministers, or agencies requesting non-genealogical research assistance.

Minister, Bishop, or Local Preacher:

*We are not the official repository for the Wesleyan Church, the AME Church, or the United Brethren in Christ (Old Constitution)
